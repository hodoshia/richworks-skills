# Sales Posts & Selling on Social

**Funnel rule that frames everything:** Instagram feed posts are the TOP of the funnel - do
not sell directly in feed posts. Selling happens in stories and DMs.

**Language:** produce output in the brand's language (default Slovak), in a plain, direct,
no-bullshit voice.

## The 3 best selling techniques in digital

### 1. Show how your product works (product videos)
Not reels - think YouTube. Having a great product is not enough; show how it works on video.
You can place the sale subtly inside the video. When a prospect asks, send them this video. To
also cover the top of the funnel (attract new clients), make how-to videos for the whole
category - e.g. a bike shop makes guides on changing a chain or pre-season maintenance.

### 2. Run campaigns properly
If you want to sell at scale, do it logically: prepare a campaign. One unified visual and one
unified message. Run the same thing everywhere and long-term (at least several months). Until
it starts annoying people, it is not a good campaign yet.

### 3. The hack to capture more attention (live / webinar)
A person gives a post ~1 second of attention, then moves on and forgets it almost immediately
- and you are trying to sell in that 1 second. What if you swap the 1-second format for one
that gets 30-60 minutes? A live video, webinar, or free live course. Live video sells, because:
- You run an educational webinar and invite an audience; whoever shows up wants your knowledge.
- At the end you can sell your add-on services.
- They will be far more interested than usual, because they just spent 30 minutes hearing about
  them.

## 7 quick online selling techniques
- Never sell in Instagram feed posts - they are the top of the funnel.
- Selling works very well in stories (e.g. share a happy client's review, with the link in the
  next story).
- Selling works in Instagram DMs - prepare message templates.
- Sell with "why", not "what".
- Facebook is currently one of the top selling channels - ads are worth it.
- Give, give, give... and only then try to sell.
- Use the link in BIO to drive people to your landing page.

## 8 tips for writing sales-post copy (with before -> after)
- **Specific:** "Last spots left!" -> "Last 5 spots!"
- **Why, not what:** "Buy this protein" -> "Thanks to this you'll build muscle"
- **Barrier-free:** "Enter your email and name" -> "Just fill this in and instantly..."
- **Close to the audience:** "We have top quality!" -> "Do you care about quality?"
- **Concrete:** "We help the planet" -> "For every item we plant a tree!"
- **Thought-through:** write 10 versions of the text, each different, then pick from them.
- **For people:** while writing, think of your audience - they are your executioner.
- **No filler:** tell people straight what you want from them, what to do for it, and how. Make
  the process easy and remove every barrier.

## How to use this skill
1. Map the ask to the funnel: top (attract) vs bottom (sell). Put selling in
   stories/DMs/live/landing, not in feed posts.
2. Pick the matching technique (product video / campaign / live / DM) for the goal.
3. When writing copy, apply all 8 copy tips and always lead with "why", be specific, and end on
   one clear, barrier-free call to action.
4. Remember the give-give-give-then-sell rhythm.
