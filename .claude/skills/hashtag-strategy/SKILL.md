# Hashtag Strategy (2026) & Banned Hashtags

**The 2026 change:** as of 1 January 2026, Instagram cut the hashtag limit from 30 to just 5.
The old "more is better" rule is gone. Hashtags are fading as a tool: they are no longer a
reliable way to get new followers, and today they can do more harm than good.

**Language:** produce output in the brand's language (default Slovak), in a plain, direct voice.

## What it means for you (quick guide)
With only 5 precious caption slots, you can't afford a mistake. If you unknowingly fill one slot
with a blacklisted word, you don't just waste space - you risk making the whole profile
invisible.

**Recommendation:** use 3 relevant, LOCAL hashtags (Slovak or Czech words). No English #books -
that exact word is blacklisted. Use a local tag instead, e.g. #knihy. Instagram's algorithm is
AI and translates SK/CZ words fine, so local tags work.

## Why Instagram bans hashtags (and shadowbans profiles)
Instagram really does block hashtags that break community rules or are tied to spam, unsuitable
content, or sensitive topics - more often than people think. It's cleaning up the space, limiting
hashtags linked to topics like:
- suicidal ideation (#suicide)
- eating disorders (#anorexia)
- explicit content (#tits #ass #youngmodel)
- spam campaigns (the classic #tagsforlikes)

The ban can be permanent or temporary (a reach limit). Either way the result is the same:
**shadowban** - posts with these tags don't show in search and effectively disappear.

**Shadowban consequences:**
- Drastic reach drop: your posts stop showing to people who don't follow you.
- Extreme/repeated cases: risk of the whole account being deleted.

The list changes dynamically - what was safe yesterday can be blacklisted today. Test a
suspicious hashtag; if it's on the list below, drop it from your next post.

## Closing principle: drop the grids, focus on content
That list is full of innocent words you could burn yourself on for nothing. The era of blindly
copying English hashtags is over.

In 2026 your strongest weapon is the text itself and SEO (keywords) spoken/shown INSIDE the
video, not in its caption. The algorithm understands context better than ever.

**The rule:** check this list, pick at most 3-5 strong LOCAL words that are genuinely yours
(e.g. a local branded tag like #mojaznackacitaknihy instead of #books), and spend the rest of
your energy on the post itself. Good content finds its way to people even without a hashtag; bad
content can't be saved by anything.

## How to use this skill
1. Cap hashtags at 5 (use ~3); prefer local SK/CZ tags over English.
2. Cross-check every intended hashtag against the banned list; drop any match.
3. Redirect effort to the content + in-video keywords/SEO, not the caption tags.
4. If reach suddenly drops, suspect a shadowban from a banned tag and audit recent posts.
