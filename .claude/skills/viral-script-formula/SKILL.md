# The "Magic Pill" Viral Script Formula

**Why it works:** most videos fail because they lecture people. This format does the opposite -
it removes the viewer's guilt and hands them an instant solution.

**Language:** produce scripts/output in the brand's language (default Slovak), in a plain,
direct voice.

## The psychological principles
1. **The Universal Trigger (mass relatability).** Name a problem the masses already know - e.g.
   "ChatGPT writes nonsense." In that second the viewer thinks "exactly, that happens to me too!"
   and stops scrolling.
2. **Guilt Removal.** Immediately add: "It's not your fault - you're just missing this setting."
   The viewer stops feeling incompetent and gets curious.
3. **The Hidden Gem (hidden truth).** No theory. Give one concrete, technical "hack" that works
   right away.
4. **Utility Viral (usefulness > entertainment).** A video like this earns far more Saves than
   Likes (e.g. 3x). The algorithm loves it: the video is perceived as a tool people return to.

## The shape: Myth -> Truth
Every example below names a relatable myth, then reveals the real hidden cause and the instant
fix. Three niches, same formula:

- **Fitness trainer:** Myth: "Your back hurts after the gym because it's weak." Truth: "It hurts
  because you deadlift without activating this one muscle. Until you rotate your elbows like this
  (demo), you're wrecking yourself."
- **Real estate agent:** Myth: "The flat isn't selling because the market is flat." Truth:
  "Nonsense. It isn't selling because your listing is missing this one keyword that investors
  have alerts set for."
- **E-shop (candles):** Myth: "A candle tunnels (burns only in the middle) because it's low
  quality." Truth: "Wrong. It tunnels because you didn't let it burn for 2 hours on the first
  light. That's the first-burn rule."

Each one contains all four principles: Universal Trigger, Guilt Removal, Hidden Gem, Utility.

## The tool: copy-paste prompt (fill one word - your Niche)
Drop this into Gemini / ChatGPT / Claude and fill your niche. The AI finds the "hidden truth"
and writes 3 viral concepts. (Slovak, verbatim.)

```
VSTUPNE DATA. Kto som, co som zac a co robim? (Niche): "XYZ"

---

Tvojou ulohou je posobit ako expert na viralnu psychologiu a manazer pozornosti. Na zaklade
vyssie zadanej Niche (oblasti) vytvor viralnu strategiu.

Aplikuj na tuto Niche presny vzorec uspesneho viralneho videa, ktory je zalozeny na troch
psychologickych spustacoch:

1. PAIN POINT (Okamzita stotoznitelnost): Identifikuj frustraciu, ktoru "kazdy pozna", ale
   mysli si, ze je to chyba nastroja alebo ich neschopnost (napr. "ChatGPT pise bludy").

2. CURIOSITY GAP (Chybajuci dielik): Tvrdenie, ze tento problem existuje LEN PRETO, ze im chyba
   jedno male, tajne "nastavenie" alebo "zmena", o ktorej nevedia (napr. "Kym nemas zapnute toto
   nastavenie...").

3. MAGIC PILL (Riesenie pre lenivych): Riesenie nesmie byt "tvrda praca". Musi to byt "jeden
   prepinac/jeden trik/jeden asset", ktory problem vyriesi OKAMZITE a zadarmo.

VYSTUP:

Vygeneruj 3 ROZNE KONCEPTY, ako tuto "Skrytu pravdu" odprezentovat. Dodrz nasledujucu strukturu:

---

KROK 1: IDENTIFIKACIA "SKRYTEJ PRAVDY"
(AI, tu najprv vymysli ten hack. Co je v tejto Niche ekvivalentom "vypnuteho nastavenia v
ChatGPT"? Musi to byt konkretny, technicky alebo hmatatelny detail, ktory meni hru.)
* Frustracia: ...
* Chybajuci "prepinac" (Hidden Gem): ...
* Vysledok po zapnuti: ...

---

KROK 2: 3 VIRALNE KONCEPTY

KONCEPT C. 1: "Nastroj je pokazeny" (The Blame Frame)
Tento koncept zbavuje divaka viny. Nastroj/metoda nefunguje nie preto, ze su hlupi, ale preto,
ze im chyba kluc.
* Pain Point (Trigger): Popis presnu situaciu, kedy to "nefunguje" (ako ked GPT pise bludy).
* Hook (Negativny/Stopka): "Pouzivat [NASTROJ/METODU] je uplna STRATA CASU!"
* Curiosity Gap: "...teda aspon dovtedy, kym si neaktivujes/nepridas TOTO."
* Magic Pill: Co presne im vo videu odhalis ako ten "chybajuci kluc"?

KONCEPT C. 2: "Robis to ako amater" (The Pro vs. Noob Frame)
Utok na ego. Rozdiel medzi masou a elitou v tejto Niche je len v tomto jednom malom triku.
* Pain Point (Trigger): Preco bezni ludia v tejto Niche zlyhavaju alebo maju priemerne vysledky?
* Hook (Stopka): "Tvoje vysledky su nanic a ani nevies preco. Chyba nie je v tebe, ale v tvojom
  'nastaveni'."
* Curiosity Gap: "Profici maju totiz zapnutu/pouzivaju jednu vec, o ktorej sa nehovori."
* Magic Pill: Odhalenie "tajneho nastavenia/ingrediencie".

KONCEPT C. 3: "Okamzita oprava" (The Instant Fix Frame)
Doraz na rychlost a lenivost. Maximalny vysledok za nulovu namahu.
* Pain Point (Trigger): Unava z toho, ako dlho trva dosiahnut vysledok starou cestou.
* Hook (Stopka): "Prestan sa trapit s [PROBLEM]. Mam na to jeden 'cheat code', ktory to vyriesi
  za 2 minuty."
* Curiosity Gap: "Vacsina ludi v tejto Niche to ma priamo pred nosom, ale ignoruju to."
* Magic Pill: Ukazka "skratky", ktora funguje hned (do 24 hodin).
```

## How to use this skill
1. Find the niche's "hidden truth": one concrete, technical, game-changing detail (the
   equivalent of "the off setting in ChatGPT") - not generic advice.
2. Script it as Myth -> relatable Trigger -> Guilt Removal -> Hidden Gem (instant hack) -> close.
3. Frame it for SAVES, not laughs - make it a tool people keep.
4. To generate options fast, run the prompt above with the user's niche and pick from the 3
   frames (Blame / Pro-vs-Noob / Instant Fix).
