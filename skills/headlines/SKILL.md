# 100 Irresistible Headline Templates

**The premise:** good headlines repeat. They keep the same form. Take the form, fill the
blanks (`___`) with whatever you want, and your irresistible headline exists.

**How to use:** pick the category that matches the goal, choose a template, fill every `___`
(and `(Cislo)` = a number) with the user's specifics. Generate several variants and let the
user choose. Keep the templates in Slovak unless the user works in another language; adapt
grammar (case/gender) so the filled headline reads naturally. Numbers in headlines work -
prefer the number category when a count fits.
