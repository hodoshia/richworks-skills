# Reel Diagnosis (AI post-mortem of a flopped video)

Turn a failed video into the next viral one. Feed the numbers AND the script to a
vision-capable AI assistant (one that can read screenshots, e.g. ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini),
ask the right question, get a diagnosis you couldn't see yourself, and rewrite. It takes ~15
minutes and saves weeks of trial and error. Data doesn't lie; AI helps you understand what the
data is saying.

**Language:** produce output in the brand's language (default Slovak), in a plain, direct voice.

## The 7 steps

### 1. Collect the data (Insights screenshots)
Open the Instagram Insights of your failed Reel and screenshot every section - capture all
screens Insights offers (it can be ~8 screenshots to cover the full analytics). The more data you
give the AI, the more precise the diagnosis.

### 2. Add the script
Numbers alone aren't enough - the AI needs to know what you said and showed to connect numbers to
content. Write or paste the FULL script including visual notes (what's on screen, sounds, on-screen
text). Example (shortened): hook "99% of people ignore this on Instagram", then 3 tips, then a CTA
to comment a keyword.

### 3. Ask the right question
This is key. Don't just say "give me tips." Ask specifically (paste verbatim, with screenshots
attached):
```
Preco tento scenar nefungoval? Na screenshotoch vidis data z Instagram Insights. Tu je scenar
videa: [vloz scenar]. Analyzuj, co zlyhalo a preco.
```
Combining data + script, the AI tells you things you wouldn't see alone, because it links the
numbers to your specific content.

### 4. Read the diagnosis (the common failure findings to look for)
Typical findings from a real flopped video - use these as a diagnostic checklist:
1. **Nobody watched to the end.** 41s video, people left at 13s. All the good stuff was at the
   end, where no one reached. -> Best material goes at the START, not the end.
2. **The hook didn't grab.** "99% of people ignore this" is a tired phrase; and an insult in
   second 3 ("your stupidity") is not a hook, it's a slap - a stranger leaves and never returns.
3. **First 10 seconds wasted.** 10s explaining where the profile is and what to click - no value,
   just app-navigation. A commenter nailed it: "I focus on the zoom more than what you're saying."
   -> Every second must give something. If the first 10 give nothing, people are gone.
4. **Too few understood the topic.** Jargon like "sales videos" and "new feature" - creators get
   it, normal people don't. Explore at 0.9% means the algorithm had no one to show it to. -> Say
   "a video that sells you" instead of "sales videos." Simple words = more people.
5. **The comments were fake.** 103 comments, nearly all the single word of a CTA keyword.
   Instagram in 2026 detects this and punishes it. 100 identical words is not engagement.
6. **The video was 2x too long.** People last ~13s on average, so make it 20s, not 42s. The main
   point must land by second 3-5.

### 5. Have the script rewritten (with AI)
Second prompt:
```
Prepis mi tento scenar tak, aby fungoval lepsie. Pouzi konkretne pravidla virality!
```
The AI returns a table that, for each original line, gives a "viral swap" (a better version) and
explains why. Examples:
- Original: "Tip one: at the end never beg for a like and follow" -> New: "Mistake #1: you beg for
  likes." (Shorter, more direct, more painful.)
- Original: "Keep only ONE masterpiece" -> New: "Keep only one video on your profile that sells
  you." (Clearer for the masses.)
Then it gives a complete new script - e.g. 21 seconds, with a hook that immediately attacks the
viewer's pain, value by second 5, and an open loop at the end.

### 6. Results (a real case)
One creator ran exactly this process on a Reel about a first-aid link that had stalled at 490
views. After getting the AI feedback and reshooting: 490 -> 182,460 views in 24 hours - 367x more.
On a "boring" topic (first aid), with no controversy, trends, or dances. 5,417 interactions, 268
new followers, and a topic she cared about finally reached people. Same process, same principle,
different account and topic, same jump.

### 7. What to take from it
This is a repeatable cycle, not luck: **shoot -> measure -> let AI analyze -> rewrite -> shoot
again.** Done deliberately and on repeat, you can make things go viral intentionally.

## Quick recap
1. Screenshot the whole Instagram Insights of your Reel (all sections).
2. Write/paste the complete script including visual notes.
3. Send both to a vision-capable AI with the step-3 question.
4. Read the diagnosis; write down the concrete lessons.
5. Reshoot from the AI's new script.

## How to use this skill
1. Make sure the AI used can read images; collect all Insights screenshots + the full script.
2. Run the diagnosis prompt, then the rewrite prompt.
3. Apply the 6-point checklist above even before asking AI, to pre-spot obvious failures.
4. Loop it: shoot -> measure -> analyze -> rewrite -> shoot.
